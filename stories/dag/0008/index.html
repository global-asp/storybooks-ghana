Bɔ ka a niŋda? What are you doing? Qu'est-ce que tu fais ?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Adam Peter Pazzack

Dagbani

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

N yiinila yila.

I am singing.

Je chante.

O gahirila o nuu.

She is waving.

Elle fait un signe de la main.

N kpahirila n nuhi.

I am clapping.

Je tape des mains.

O teerila o bɔɣiri.

She is stretching.

Elle s’étire.

O boona.

He is calling.

Il appelle quelqu’un.

O labisiri bɔhigu.

I am answering.

Je réponds.

O wumda.

She is listening.

Elle écoute.

Bɔ ka a niŋda?

What are you doing?

Qu’est-ce que tu fais ?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Adam Peter Pazzack
Language: Dagbani
Level: Level 1
